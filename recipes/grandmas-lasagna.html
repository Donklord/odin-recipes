Grandma's Lasagna

Description

Straight from my wifes grandma's recipe book. A no-frills sraight by the books lasagna recipe, the way god intended it. No veggies, plently of meat and cheese.

What you will need

  • 9x13 baking dish
  • 3/4 cup parmesian cheese
  • Lasagna noodles
  • 1 cup cottage cheese (small curd)
  • 16 oz mozzarella cheese (low moisture)
  • 2lbs ground beef (85% lean)
  • 1/2 cup chopped onion
  • 1 teaspoon garlic salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon oregano
  • 2 cans pizza sauce (10.5 oucnes each, or one large can)
  • 1 can tomato paste (4 oz)

Steps

  1. Prehat oven to 350 degrees f
  2. Boil 9-12 lasanga noodles and rinse
  3. Fry hamburger and onions, drain off grease. Then add 1 can pizza sauce, 1 can tomato paste, garlic salt, oregano, simmer 15 minutes. Once done, take off heat, let cool and add cottage cheese.
  4. Layer lasagna
    1. Layer 1 - noodles, meat, parmesian cheese, mozzarella cheese
    2. Layer 2 - noodles, meat, parmesian cheese, mozzarella cheese
    3. Layer 3 - noodles, meat, parmesian cheese, 1 can pizza sauce, mozzarella cheese
  5. Bake for 30 minutes
