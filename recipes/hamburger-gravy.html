Hamburger Gravy

Description

Classic hamburger gravy recipe, another classic grandma recipe. Too bad I cant get my wife to eat this more. Eat over mashed potatoes for an instant classic.

What you will need

  • 1 can condensed mushroom soup
  • 1 can condensed cream of celery soup
  • 2 lbs ground beef (85% lean)
  • 1 can milk
  • 1 yellow onion (medium, diced)

Steps

  1. Brown beef in a scauce pan with yellow onion
  2. Drain greese
  3. Mix in cream of mushroom soup, cream of celery soup and milk
  4. Serve over baked, or mashed potatoes
