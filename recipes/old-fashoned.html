Grandma's Recipes

Old Fashoned

Description

A classic drink, in the Wisconsin style. Choose from sweet or sour, with whiskey or brandy. When ordered from a bar in wisconsin you would say "whiskey sweet".

What you will need

  • Rocks glass with ice
  • Cocktail skewer
  • Bitters
  • Sugar cube
  • 2 ounces whiskey or brandy
  • Sprite or Squirt
  • Olives or marashino cherries

Steps

Your mix will vary based on what type of old fashoned you want (whiskey or brandy, sweet or sour).

  1. Add sugar cube to glass, douse with 3-4 drops of bitters and muddle together.
  2. Add in 2 ounces of the liquor of your choise (whiskey or brandy)
  3. Finish the drink, based on your choise of sweet our sour
  4. Sweet
    1. Add ice
    2. Mix top off with sprite
    3. Add cherries to a skewer, and to drink
    4. Add drink straw
  5. Sour
    1. Add ice
    2. Mix top off with squirt
    3. Add olives to a skewer, and to drink
    4. Add drink straw
